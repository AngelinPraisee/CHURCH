WELCOME TO OUR CHURCH

New Life Church, Avadi

A Christ-centered church, with a rich legacy of over 64 years, rooted in faith, worship, and prayer. Led by Pastor Jacob Koshy, we are a loving
community committed to sharing God's word, growing together in faith, and reflecting His love to all. At the heart of our faith is the belief that God’s love is unchanging and available to all. We hold the teachings of the Bible as the foundation for our lives and seek to follow Christ’s example in everything we do. Our services, ministries, and programs are designed to nurture spiritual growth and encourage lifelong discipleship. We are more than just a church; we are a family. Here, you will find people who care, support one another, and walk alongside each other in life’s joys and challenges. From children to seniors, newcomers to long-time members, we welcome you to join us in worship, service, and fellowship.

OUR VISION

To see people passionately devoted to God.

Our vision is to see people passionately devoted to God, living lives transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit. We desire to build a Christ-centered community rooted in worship, prayer, and the teaching of God’s Word, where every individual grows in faith, discovers their God-given purpose, and walks daily in His love. Through obedience to Scripture and the leading of the Holy Spirit, we seek to impact families, communities, and
generations for the glory of God.

ABOUT OUR CHURCH

New Life Church

Our Church is a Christ-centered community built on faith, prayer and the unchanging Word of God. Established over 60 years ago, our church stands as a living testimony to God’s faithfulness across generations. What began as a small step of obedience has grown into a spiritual home where believers gather to worship, grow and serve together as one body in Christ. Through every season, God has been our strength, guide and provider.

One body in Christ

Senior Pastor

Pr. JACOB KOSHY

Pastor K.G. Koshy founded the church in Nov 1 1961. After his death in 1995, Pastor Jacob Koshy is serving as the Senior Pastor of New Life Church, Avadi. He completed his Bible Studies in Elim Bible College, USA. He is a devoted servant of God, who leads the church with faith, humility, and a deep passion for prayer and the word of God. With over 34 years of pastoral Service, he has been shepherding the church with unwavering faith, humility, and a deep commitment to the word of God. Through his leadership, the church has grown spiritually while remaining rooted in its strong foundation and values passed down through generations.
